// How to Play
🎯
Your Mission
You are an Equinix reseller on a first discovery call with Dr. Rachel Kim, VP of Infrastructure at Meridian Health Network. Ask smart questions to build a complete picture — including a critical need she hasn't mentioned.
⏱
5-Minute Clock
A real discovery call has time pressure. The countdown starts when you begin. When time runs out the session ends automatically. Every question counts — make them sharp.
💡
Hints Available
You have 3 hints available in the sidebar. Each hint costs 5 points from your final score. Use them strategically — don't burn them early.
📊
How You're Scored
Seven discovery dimensions need to be uncovered. The hidden need is the hardest to find and makes the biggest difference. Watch Dr. Kim's mood — it signals how well your questions are landing.
// Rules of Engagement
01
Lead with workload, not colocation. Don't let the customer's surface ask define the conversation. Dig into what their systems actually do.
02
Ask one focused question at a time. Dr. Kim is busy and guarded. Broad or multi-part questions get short answers.
03
Watch her mood indicator. It shows how engaged or impatient she's getting. Irrelevant questions will cost you her attention.
04
The hidden need won't surface on its own. You must ask the right question to unlock it. Think about what a healthcare enterprise expanding into new markets might be worried about.
05
Type your own questions. This is a practice environment — the more authentic your questions, the more you will learn from the debrief.
